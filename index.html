Our Story

Founded in 2015, The Daily Grind began as a dream to create more than just a coffee shop‚Äîwe wanted to build a community hub where neighbors become friends and every cup tells a story.

Our founder, Maria Santos, spent years traveling the world, learning from master roasters and baristas. She brought that knowledge home to Massachusetts, sourcing beans directly from sustainable farms and perfecting the art of the perfect brew.

Today, we're proud to serve over 500 cups of happiness daily, support local farmers, and provide a warm, welcoming space for our community to gather, work, and connect.

What Sets Us Apart

Our commitment to excellence in every cup

Sustainably Sourced

We partner directly with coffee farmers who practice sustainable, ethical farming. Every bean is traceable, fair-trade, and chosen for its exceptional quality and the positive impact it creates.

Expert Craftsmanship

Our baristas are trained artisans who understand the science and art of coffee. From precise temperature control to latte art, every detail matters in creating your perfect cup.

Community First

More than a coffee shop, we're your neighborhood gathering place. We host local artists, support community events, and create a space where everyone feels welcome and valued.
